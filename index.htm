Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers

We are dedicated to understanding and solving your language communication needs. Get your questions answered right away.

We provide interpretation services for a wide range of languages including English, French, Kinyarwanda, Swahili, Arabic, Chinese, German, Spanish, and many more. Our certified interpreters are native speakers or highly proficient in their language pairs. Whether you need interpretation for business meetings, medical appointments, legal proceedings, or casual conversations, we have the right interpreter for you.

We offer flexible booking options to meet your needs. For urgent requests, we can arrange an interpreter within 1-2 hours. For scheduled appointments, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability of your preferred interpreter. You can book through our website, WhatsApp, phone call, or email. Our 24/7 availability means we're always ready to help with your language needs.

Virtual interpretation (OPI/VRI) is conducted via Zoom or Google Meet, perfect for remote meetings, international calls, or when physical presence isn't possible. On-site interpretation involves a professional interpreter physically present at your location, ideal for face-to-face meetings, medical appointments, legal proceedings, or events where personal interaction is important. Both services maintain the same high quality and confidentiality standards.

Our pricing is competitive and transparent. Interpretation services start from $25/hour, document translation from $0.10/word, and transcription from $1.50/minute. Prices vary based on language pair, urgency, and service type. We offer special rates for NGOs, regular clients, and bulk orders. Contact us for a personalized quote, and we'll provide detailed pricing with no hidden fees.

Translation turnaround times depend on document length and complexity. Standard translations (up to 1000 words) are typically completed within 2-3 business days. Rush services are available for urgent documents with same-day or next-day delivery. We also offer express services for larger projects.
